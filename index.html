JuliaQuantum

Julia libraries for Quantum

We are initializing JuliaQuantum organization

To join our organization, please contact i2000s AT hotmail DOT com with your GitHub ID and a proposal of possible contributions in JuliaQuantum. Our organization on GitHub can be found in https://github.com/JuliaQuantum/. Comprehensive discussions can be found in the issue page linked from the project hosting page.

Now our libraries are likely to reach

  • Basic quantum states and operations;
  • Quantum chemistry;
  • Quantum Lindblad dynamics;
  • Quantum tensor network (product states);
  • Quantum information and computation;
  • Quantum measurement and control...
The topics and styles will strongly depend on our developers. Hopefully you can bring your specialty into our open source projects on GitHub.
