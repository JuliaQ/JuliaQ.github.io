Julia Libraries for Quantum Science and Technology

Updates:

There will be another Julia user meetup in the APS March meeting--announced in this post.

The videos and slides for the Julia user meetup hosted by us at Berkeley on Feb 21, 2015 are now online! Abstracts for each talk is in this post. There are three invited speakers on optimization, quantum statistics and parallel computing.

The QuantumBase open-source project has been launched on GitHub.

Check out other announcements and discussions on GitHub to find out the overall roadmap and progress.

Now our libraries are likely to include:

  • Basic Julia typing system for quantum states and operations;
  • Quantum dynamics solvers in Julia;
  • Application packages in quantum chemistry, quantum information and computation, quantum metrology and control...
Feel free to distribute and edit our open source projects on GitHub.
